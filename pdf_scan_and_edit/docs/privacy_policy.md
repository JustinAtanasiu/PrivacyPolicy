# Privacy Policy for myPDF: Offline Scanner & Edit

**Last Updated: August 18, 2025**

## Overview

myPDF is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information when you use our services.

## Data Collection and Usage

### No Data Collection
myPDF operates with a **privacy-first approach**:
- **No personal information is collected**
- **No usage analytics or tracking**
- **No account creation required**
- **No data transmission to external servers**

### Local Processing Only
All document processing occurs entirely on your device:
- **PDF editing and viewing** happens locally
- **Document scanning and OCR** uses device-only processing
- **Image enhancement and filtering** performed on-device
- **File management** accesses only your local storage

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions solely for core functionality:

### Camera Access
- **Purpose**: Document scanning and photo capture for PDF creation
- **Usage**: Only when you actively use the scan feature
- **Storage**: Photos are processed locally and optionally saved to your device

### Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: Import existing photos for PDF conversion
- **Usage**: Only when you select "Choose from Photos"
- **Scope**: Access limited to photos you explicitly select

### File System Access
- **Purpose**: Reading, editing, and saving PDF documents
- **Usage**: Manage your PDF files and documents
- **Scope**: Only files you choose to open or locations you select for saving

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only
- All documents remain on your device
- No cloud backup or synchronization
- Files are stored in standard iOS/Android document directories
- You maintain full control over your documents

### Temporary Files
- Temporary files may be created during PDF processing
- These are automatically deleted after processing completes
- No temporary data persists between app sessions

## Third-Party Services

### No External Services
- No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage integration

### Offline Operation
- App functions completely offline
- No internet connection required for any features
- Your documents never leave your device

## Security

### Device Security
- All data processing uses standard iOS/Android security frameworks
- Document access follows platform security guidelines
- No custom encryption needed as data never leaves your device

### App Security
- Regular security updates through app store updates
- No user accounts or passwords to manage
- No network-based attack vectors

## Children's Privacy

myPDF does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be safely used by users of all ages as all processing occurs locally on the device.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted in app updates with the revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the app store review system or our support channels.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data:
- There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
- No data portability issues exist
- You maintain complete control over all documents on your device

---

**Key Principle**: Your privacy is protected because your data never leaves your device. myPDF processes everything locally, ensuring your documents and personal information remain completely private and secure.
